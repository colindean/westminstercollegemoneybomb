We Need Your Participation

Organizations can always use more money, but what Westminster College needs is your participation. Westminster already has an above average rate of alumni participation: more than 26% of alumni gave something last year, even $1. Compare that to a national average of 9%.

Having greater participation means that Westminster gets access to better grants and better programs, so students, faculty, and staff can reap the benefits and improve the mission of Westminster College.

Did you know?

  • Only 70% of a student's tuition covers the cost of funding their education.
  • Donations from alumni and friends cover the remaining 30%.
  • No tuition money is used for capital improvements.
